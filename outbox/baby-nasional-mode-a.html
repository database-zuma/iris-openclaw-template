Strategic Insights

📊 Tier Distribution

  • T1 + T2 = 77,4% volume (33.496 prs), backbone kategori
  • T3 (11.687 prs) masih potensial, perlu push lebih agresif
  • T4 (6.220 prs, 15 artikel) — volume per artikel rendah, review assortment
  • T8 premium (638 prs, ASP Rp 294K) — niche but healthy margin

🏆 Product Winners

  • BABY CLASSIC 4 = top seller (2.622 prs), CLASSIC seri terlaris
  • VELCRO BRICKS series (4 SKU) total 7.710 prs — best-in-class
  • STITCH premium play: 2 artikel, ASP Rp 222K, clear margin leader
  • Disney collabs menyumbang 36% total — partnership strategy proven

📈 Seasonal Patterns

  • Peak: Dec (5.198) dan Mar (4.181) — school holiday & Lebaran effect
  • Low: Feb (1.966) dan Jun (2.581) — perlu activation program
  • Q4 growth Nov→Dec (+34%) paling agresif, manfaatkan dengan promo timing
  • Jan'26 vs Jan'25 +11,3% YoY — tren positif masih terjaga

🗺️ Geographic Opportunities

  • Jakarta ASP tertinggi (Rp 158K) vs rata-rata Rp 143K — premium market
  • Sumatra + Batam hanya 826 prs combined — under-penetrated
  • Bali per-store avg: 721 prs, Jatim: 1.130 prs — Jatim lebih produktif per toko
  • Lombok (2.313 prs dari 2 toko) = hidden champion, 1.157 prs/toko
